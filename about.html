About Us

At "Leben", we believe travel isn't just about visiting a place; it's about the feeling it leaves in your soul. we don't just book flights; we craft journeys that connect people to local culture, adventure, or profound relaxation.

History

Leben travels started in 2018 with one belief: exploring the world shouldn't be stressful or only for the wealthy.

Our founder, after a frustrating trip booking experience with hidden fees and zero local support, set out to build an agency that puts people first. what began as booking budget flights for friends in Enugu has grown into a full-service agency helping 2,000+ travelers explore Nigeria and 15+ countries.

We've weathered cancelled flights, visa policy changes, and the 2020 travel shutdown. Each challenge taught us how to pivot fast and protect our clients. Today we blend local expertise with global partners to make travel smoother.
